Audit Risk

Risk of expressing an inappropriate opinion

Meaning of Audit Risk

Defined in SA 200.

Audit risk is the risk that the auditor expresses an inappropriate audit opinion when the financial statements are materially misstated.

Components of Audit Risk

Three elements.

Audit risk consists of:
  • Inherent Risk
  • Control Risk
  • Detection Risk

Reduction of Audit Risk

Not elimination.

Audit risk can be reduced but cannot be eliminated completely due to inherent limitations of audit.

Exam Perspective

High scoring area.

Always conclude:
Audit risk can be reduced to an acceptably low level, but not eliminated.
