Conduct of Audit as per Standards on Auditing

Mandatory compliance framework

Compliance with SAs

Mandatory requirement.

The auditor shall comply with all Standards on Auditing that are relevant to the audit engagement.

When Is an SA Relevant?

Two conditions.

An SA is relevant when:
  • The SA is applicable to the audit
  • The conditions addressed by the SA exist

Understanding the Entire SA

Not just the main paragraphs.

The auditor must understand:
  • Objective of the SA
  • Requirements
  • Application and explanatory material

Claim of Compliance

Important restriction.

The auditor shall not represent compliance with Standards on Auditing unless all relevant SAs have been complied with.

Exam Perspective

Very scoring area.

Always write:
Compliance with SAs is mandatory and not optional.
