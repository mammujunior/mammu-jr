Scope of SA 200

How SA 200 fits into the audit framework

Applicability

Universal standard.

SA 200 applies to all audits of financial statements and is relevant for every audit engagement.

Framework Standard

Base of all SAs.

SA 200 provides the overall framework for understanding and applying all other Standards on Auditing.

Link with Other SAs

Works together.

SA 200 works in conjunction with:
  • SA 210 – Engagement Terms
  • SA 300 – Audit Planning
  • SA 315 & 330 – Risk Assessment & Response
  • SA 700 series – Audit Reporting

Exam Perspective

How to conclude answers.

Always conclude:
SA 200 provides the foundation and framework for all other SAs.
