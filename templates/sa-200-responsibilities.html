Auditor vs Management Responsibility

Clear division of roles in an audit

Management’s Responsibility

Primary responsibility.

Management is responsible for:
  • Preparation of financial statements
  • Designing and maintaining internal controls
  • Prevention and detection of fraud and error

Auditor’s Responsibility

Independent examination.

The auditor’s responsibility is to:
  • Obtain reasonable assurance
  • Express an opinion on financial statements
  • Comply with Standards on Auditing

Important Distinction

Very important for exams.

The auditor is not responsible for the preparation of financial statements.

Preparation is the responsibility of management.

Exam Perspective

Common mistake.

Do not write:
❌ “Auditor prepares or corrects accounts”

Always clearly separate responsibilities.
