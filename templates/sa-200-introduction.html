Introduction of SA 200

The foundation standard for all audits

Purpose of SA 200

Why this standard exists.

SA 200 establishes the overall objectives of the independent auditor and explains the nature and scope of an audit conducted in accordance with Standards on Auditing.

Key Areas Covered

What SA 200 explains.

SA 200 explains:
  • Overall objectives of the auditor
  • Nature and scope of an audit
  • Reasonable assurance
  • Professional judgment and skepticism
  • Compliance with Standards on Auditing

Applicability

Where SA 200 applies.

SA 200 applies to all audits of financial statements and provides the framework within which all other Standards on Auditing operate.

Exam Perspective

How to approach questions.

In exams, SA 200 introduction answers should:
  • Highlight it as a framework standard
  • Mention applicability to all audits
  • Link it with other SAs
